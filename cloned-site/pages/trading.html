We specialize in blending fuel oil to cater to a wide range of market requirements. Our sophisticated blending operations provide fuel for our bunker barges, ensuring efficient marine fuel distribution. We also supply utility-grade fuel oil to power plants, supporting energy generation across various sectors. Additionally, our blending capabilities enable us to leverage arbitrage opportunities, facilitating the movement of larger volumes across vessels and markets. We trade two key grades of fuel oil: high sulfur and low sulfur, with our low sulfur fuel oil primarily derived from end products produced at our Fujairah refinery. This allows us to meet the growing demands of the low sulfur market while adapting to diverse regulatory and market needs. This strategic flexibility enhances our ability to meet global demand while optimizing profitability through targeted fuel blending and distribution.

managing the transportation, storage, and supply of a broad portfolio of
products from source to end consumers. With extensive industry experience,
